Our History

Olive Grove is a charming restaurant that traces its roots back to the early 1980s, when it first opened its doors as a family-owned Mediterranean eatery. Nestled in a quaint location, the restaurant quickly became a local favorite, renowned for its authentic Mediterranean cuisine, warm ambiance, and exceptional service. Over the years, Olive Grove has stayed true to its traditional recipes, using only the freshest ingredients and finest olive oils, sourced directly from the Mediterranean region. The menu features a delightful array of dishes, from savory kebabs and fresh seafood to vibrant salads and homemade baklava, all crafted with a passion for culinary excellence. With its cozy atmosphere and dedication to quality, Olive Grove continues to be a beloved dining destination for both locals and visitors alike.
